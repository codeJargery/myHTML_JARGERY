FORMATTING

Next, let's take a look at paragrah, bold, emphasis, underline, italic, and strikethrough.

This is a paragrah denoted with the paragrah tag (<p></p>)

We use the bold tag (<b>) to bold a text like you're seeing.You can also use <strong> to bolden a text, but they do not have the same meaning.

The <em> element is also used to Emphasize a text.You can also use the <i> to do same.

They do not have the same meaning. The first holds a semantic meaning whiles the later is just for presentation.


The <s> is used to strikethrough a text. You can also use the <del> to display a deleted text.They all have an opening and closing tags.

Another element is the mark (<mark>) element. It is used to highlight a text.


The <u> is used to underline a text. Although the <ins>can do similar task, they do not hold the same meaning.

The first is just for presentation whiles the later holds a semantic meaning-it is used to display an inserted text.


The superscript (<sup>) and subscript (<sub>) is used to display mathematical and scientific figures like these 328, H2O respectively.

Others include <blockquote></blockquote>,<abbr></abbr>, <cite></cite>, etc.
